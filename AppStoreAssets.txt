DESCRIPTION (up to 4000 chars)
-------------------------------
Chase the Light is the essential companion for photographers who live and breathe
natural light. Whether you're planning a sunrise portrait session, scouting a
golden-hour landscape, or hunting the perfect blue-hour cityscape shot — Chase
the Light tells you exactly when every magical light phase begins, how long it
lasts, and what it's best suited for.

REAL-TIME SUN TRACKING
The animated sun arc shows you precisely where the sun sits right now, arcing
from sunrise to sunset with your current altitude displayed live. At a glance you
know whether you have minutes or hours before the light changes.

SIX LIGHT PHASES — FULLY TIMED
• Astronomical Twilight (-18° → -12°) — Stars brilliant, sky still dark
• Nautical Twilight (-12° → -6°) — Deep violet blue bleeds to the horizon
• Blue Hour (-6° → 0°) — Even, shadowless, cool blue light
• Golden Hour (0° → 6°) — Warm directional light, long dramatic shadows
• Civil Twilight (6° → 12°) — Soft diffuse warmth, gentle contrast
• Soft Light (12° → 45°) — Full color, subtle texture, gentle glow

Each phase shows morning and evening windows with exact start/end times, duration
in minutes, and photography specialties (portraits, landscapes, astrophoto, and
more). Active phases glow so you never miss the moment.

LIVE SATELLITE VIEW
Pull up a real-time GOES-East GeoColor satellite image refreshed every 15 minutes.
See cloud cover and weather patterns across the continent at a glance — no more
stepping outside to discover an overcast sky. Tap for a full-screen view.

DYNAMIC SKY BACKGROUNDS
The app background shifts in real time with the sun — deep midnight navy at night,
purple-to-amber twilight, warm golden hour tones, and crisp blue daylight sky.
The interface always matches the light outside your window.

PRIVACY FIRST
Location is used only while the app is open and never stored or shared. No account
required. No ads. No tracking.

PERFECT FOR
• Landscape and travel photographers
• Portrait photographers chasing the golden glow
• Astrophotographers timing their night sessions
• Videographers and cinematographers
• Anyone who wants to be outside at the right moment

REQUIRED BEFORE SUBMISSION
---------------------------
1. Add 1024×1024 AppIcon PNG(s) to:
   SunAndSky/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/
   (Contents.json already has the correct structure for standard, dark, and tinted icons)
   File names expected: AppIcon.png, AppIcon-dark.png, AppIcon-tinted.png

2. Sign the app with your Apple Developer account in Xcode:
   Signing & Capabilities → Team → Haupt2it (or your registered team)

3. Create a Privacy Policy page and add its URL in App Store Connect

4. Entitlements: Location When In Use only (no background modes needed)

5. Export Compliance: No encryption used → answer "No" to encryption questions

CAPABILITIES USED
-----------------
• CoreLocation (WhenInUse) — sun/phase calculations for current position
• Network access — GOES satellite image download from cdn.star.nesdis.noaa.gov
• MapKit — reverse geocoding for place name display
